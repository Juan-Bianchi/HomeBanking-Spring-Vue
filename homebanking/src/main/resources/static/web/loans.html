Personal loans

Automotive loans
  • Loan amounts from $30,000 to $300,000
  • Fixed rates of 7% - 20% APR
  • Submit your application online and get a response in less than 60 seconds
  • Once approved, get funds same day to your account, and up to 2 business days for non-Mindhub Bros accounts, upon approval and when using direct deposit.
  • You can repay your loan over 6, 12, 24, or 36 months.
    Mortgages
  • Loan amounts from $100,000 to $500,000
